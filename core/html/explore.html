About Our Department

Department Overview

Studying computer science develops analytical and problem-solving skills fundamental to a huge range of careers in computing and beyond. India has the largest and fastest growing IT sector, the revenue for this sector grows by 7.7 % every year! In a year, the industry hires over 205,000 new talent in India alone, to cope with the demand, many more young minds are needed. There is no dearth of opportunities and no limit for growth when you study this course. With this course, you can join the most sought after industry

Department Mission

MD 1
To provide State-of-the-art Infrastructure Facilities
MD 2
To provide exposure to the latest tools in the area of Computer Hardware and Software
MD 3
To strive for Academic Excellence through Research in Computer Science and Engineering with creative Teaching-Learning Pedagogy
MD 4
To establish Industry Institute Interaction and make students ready for the Industrial Environment
MD 5
To transform students into Entrepreneurial, Technically Competent, Socially Responsible and Ethical Computer Science Professional
